Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in this experiment. As part of this experiment, you will be copying and pasting phrases, sentences, and paragraphs of texts using 2 different methods: Crtl + V followed by Crlt + C, and AutoComPaste, a new technique that has been developed. The data we get from your participation will be very useful for us in evaluating both methods.

This experiment will take no longer than 15 minutes. It will consist of 3 steps:

  1. Pre-experiment questionnaire
  2. Experiment trials
  3. Post-experiment questionnaire

During this experiment, there will be csv files that are downloaded. Please send those files to your researcher when you are done.
